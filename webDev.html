Reflection

We were task to assign ourselves the roles that are given in the instructions. Luckily, I was assigned as Frontend Developer. We first held a group meeting to discuss the project timeline, deliverables, software applications to use,and as well as task designation. Our leader (backend developer), first drafted a simple logic to quiz website, and I drafted a low fidelity prototype since I'm not really that good in designs. With this drafts, our UI/UX designer made the high fidelity prototype for the frontend developers to follow. Then, we started coding from the frontend to the backend and held meetings for the progress update. At the end, our leader compiled and finalized our website making sure everything works perfectly fine.

REFLECTION

Before the semester began, I was filled with excitement and anticipation to delve into web development and start creating amazing, functional, and visually appealing websites. The idea of bringing creative ideas to life on the web was incredibly motivating for me. As we progressed through the semester and completed the midterms, I can confidently say that I’ve built a strong enough foundation in HTML and CSS, which are the essential building blocks of web development. However, I’ve come to realize that web development is a journey of continuous learning, and there is still a lot to master.

When it comes to JavaScript, I’ve only scratched the surface, and I recognize the need to invest more time in understanding its programming logic and concepts. I’ve also learned the importance of debugging, problem-solving, and thinking creatively to overcome challenges in coding. This has taught me patience and resilience, qualities I believe will benefit me beyond web development. While I feel proud of how far I’ve come, I know there’s still a lot more I can learn to transform my ideas into fully functional projects. This semester has reinforced my enthusiasm for web development and has given me a clearer picture of the skills and dedication it requires. Overall, the journey so far has been rewarding, and I’m looking forward to exploring advanced topics, honing my skills, and achieving my goal of becoming a competent web developer.
